Upload your books here!!

What we will be following over here is a specific kind of barter system, that focuses only on books. Yes, so the only thing that would be running this website is books(not ebooks). So upload the books that you feel you can part with, and share the joy of reading!!

What do you have to upload?
Well, you have to upload the cover photo of your book, along with it's images from 6 angles. Feels tough? Don't worry the following guidelines will make you more than ready :
  1. This is the most important tip that you should not at all avoid and it's simply that your book should not be having more than 50% damage.Simply put, your book should still be in a condition that the person who obtains your book, can be able to read without frustration! I know 50% sounds vague, so here are the tips that you could follow :
    1. Try not to add books with torn edges/covers.It makes reading and handling the book difficult. The left side book is something which is unacceptable(better if given to scrap dealers).Books with dog ears will be accepted.
    2. Books with torn pages won't be accepted, rather if the page binding is removed(upto a manageable extent) it still is better to be uploaded!
    3. Markings are something that many love to make on books(don't count me among them) Be it to simplify meaning or any other stuff, their won't be any trouble as long as the text in the book is legible!
  2. The thumbnail that you post for the book, can be a picture that you have of that book from Google,Bing,Yahoo,etc.
    • Try to use the same picture that you have on your hardcopy cover.It helps generate trust over you.
