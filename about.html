About Hardcopy

For whom is Hardcopy.com?
Hardcopy.com is a website for all those who love reading, the traditional style i.e. by feeling those pages while reading, the smell of the pages while navigating through them, and of course saving your eyes. And apart of that, you don't have to pay any cost of the book, only it's delivery charge that would be really trivial.
Our Mission :
To act as an intermediary for all book lovers, who want to read books in a traditional manner, the same way you read ebooks. All those who are ready to part with their books can upload their books to Hardcopy.In the same manner anybody who wants to read can order the book from the genres they like.
How to select books?
The home page contains all the genres that we offer. Just go to the homepage select a genre that you like, and then, you will be redirected to all the books related to that specific genre. Just select your book and that's it! You will be shipped your book as early as possible, and all you have to pay is just 40₹ as the delivery charge
